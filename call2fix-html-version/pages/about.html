Our Story

Call2Fix was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional plumbing and electrical services to the residents and businesses of Cape Town. With 7+ years of experience, we've built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

We believe that quality workmanship and honest communication are the foundation of lasting customer relationships. Every member of our team is committed to delivering exceptional service, whether it's a small repair or a major installation.

From emergency burst pipes to complete bathroom renovations, we have the expertise and experience to handle any plumbing or electrical challenge.
